“You are a junior developer at a small games studio. You have developed a tool, service, plugin, gameplay system, or multiplayer feature. Your lead has asked you to produce a short proposal explaining what would be required to support this work in a real production environment.”

Activity Submission

- a short explanation of the tool, service, plugin, or mechanic you developed

- the technical stack needed to support it in production

- the hardware and/or cloud requirements needed to run, host, test, or maintain it

- a rough estimate of likely costs

    - initial setup costs

     - ongoing costs such as hosting, storage, build systems, or server usage

- the target platform(s) it would need to support

- a simple diagram showing how the system would function in practice

- if your work involved gameplay, connected systems, or multiplayer, also consider:

     - server hosting

     - networking

     - backend services

     - live support or maintenance costs
